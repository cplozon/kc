Sport Massage and Therapeutic Massage

Randori Bodywork knows athletes. In fact in less than 8 years we have provided sports massage at over 50 major sporting events taking care of athletes in all stages of competition. These events range from collegiate swim meets, triathlons, and the 2016 Summer Olympic Games.

You don’t have to be an athlete to see us though. If you want less pain, freer movement, or maybe you’re tired of the conventional spa setting, we’re here for you too.

We also implement a variety of techniques to ensure we deliver the best session possible for our clients. Sport massage sessions can include deep tissue, myofascial release, cupping, instrument assisted soft tissue mobilization (IASTM or Gua Sha), RockTape kinesiology taping , and corrective exercise. Find out more on our Sport Massage page.

We deliver our bodywork sessions in two locations, CrossFit Durham in downtown Durham and CrossFit Homeward in Chapel Hill. Click the links to find out more about our locations.
